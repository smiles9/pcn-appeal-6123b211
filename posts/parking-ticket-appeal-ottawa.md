# How to Appeal a Parking Ticket in Ottawa

If you’ve received a parking ticket in Canada's capital, you don't have to pay it immediately. The City of Ottawa uses an **Administrative Penalty System (APS)**, which means you can request a review (screening) by an official without going to court.

## Ottawa Parking Ticket Hotspots
Certain areas in Ottawa are heavily monitored by by-law officers. Watch out when parking near these landmarks:
*   **Lansdowne Park (TD Place):** Extremely high enforcement during RedBlacks games or 67s matches.
*   **ByWard Market:** Tight parking rules and active enforcement late into the evening.
*   **Parliament Hill / Downtown Core:** Strict "No Stopping" and "No Standing" zones that lead to immediate ticketing.
*   **Carleton University:** Lot 5 and campus roads are frequent targets for parking infractions.

## How to Dispute Your Ticket
Ottawa offers a straightforward online screening process. You must act within **15 days** of receiving the notice to avoid additional fees.

1.  **Online Review:** Visit the [City of Ottawa Parking Portal](https://ottawa.ca/en/parking-roads-and-travel/tickets/parking-tickets) and select "Request a Review."
2.  **Submit Evidence:** Upload photos of signs, your parking permit, or proof of payment (Pay-and-Display receipt or PayByPhone app screenshot).
3.  **Screening Decision:** A Screening Officer will review your case. They can uphold, reduce, or cancel the fine.

## Common Grounds for Appeal
| Ground | What to Prove |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Broken Meter** | Provide the meter number and a photo of the "Out of Order" screen. |
| **Confusing Signage** | Show that signs were obscured by trees, snow, or were contradictory. |
| **Loading/Unloading** | Proof of commercial activity if ticketed in a loading zone. |

## FAQ
|A| Can I ignore an Ottawa parking ticket? ||| No. Unpaid tickets will prevent you from renewing your vehicle permit and may be sent to collections.
|A| How long does the appeal take? ||| Most online screening reviews are completed within 10 to 30 business days.
|A| What if I lose the screening? ||| You can request a further review by a Hearing Officer, which is the final stage of the Administrative Penalty System.
