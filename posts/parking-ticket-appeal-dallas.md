# Dallas Parking Ticket Appeal Guide

Received a parking citation in the Big D? Don't pay it immediately. Dallas has a specific administrative hearing process that allows you to contest unfair or incorrect tickets.

## 1. Identify Your Citation Type

Most Dallas parking tickets are civil violations. You have **15 days** from the date of issuance to either pay or contest the ticket before late fees are applied.

## 2. Top Ticket Hotspots in Dallas

*   **Deep Ellum:** High enforcement during evening hours and weekends. Watch for "No Parking" signs near music venues.

*   **Victory Park:** Heavily patrolled during events at the American Airlines Center.

*   **Lower Greenville:** Strict residential permit zones and valet-only lanes.

*   **Uptown:** Meter expiration is the #1 cause of tickets here.

## 3. The 3-Step "Deep Strike" Defense (Appeal to Court) (Step-by-Step)

1.  **Online Portal:** Visit the [Dallas Dispute Citation System](https://prdwmq.etimspayments.com/pbw/include/dallas/dispute_request.jsp).

2.  **Enter Details:** You will need your citation number and vehicle registration (license plate).

3.  **Submit Evidence:** Upload photos of signs, broken meters, or your valid permit.

4.  **Administrative Hearing:** If the online dispute is rejected, you can request an in-person or mail-in hearing with the Adjudication Office.

## 4. Common Valid Defense Strategies

*   **Broken Meter:** If the meter was malfunctioning, take a video of it failing to accept payment.

*   **Missing Signs:** If the signage was obscured by trees or vandalized, you have strong grounds for dismissal.

*   **Vehicle Sold:** If you no longer owned the vehicle, submit a Texas Motor Vehicle Transfer Notification.
