# Perth Parking Ticket Appeal Guide (2026)

If you've received a parking infringement notice in the City of Perth, you have options. Whether it's a council-issued fine or a private parking invoice, this guide covers the latest 2026 rules for a successful appeal.

## Perth Parking Hotspots
Stay vigilant in these high-enforcement zones:
- **Elizabeth Quay**: Heavy monitoring for drop-off and short-stay violations.
- **Northbridge (James St & Lake St)**: Strict evening enforcement for entertainment precinct parking.
- **St Georges Terrace**: Rapid ticketing for loading zone and clearway infringements during business hours.
- **Kings Park**: Enforcement of time limits near major lookouts.

## How to Appeal a City of Perth Infringement
Council tickets should be appealed via the official City of Perth portal.
1. **Internal Review**: Request an initial review based on administrative error or valid mitigating circumstances.
2. **Evidence**: Attach clear photos of signage, ticket machines, or your vehicle's position.
3. **Court Election**: If the review is rejected, you can elect to have the matter heard in the Magistrates Court (legal costs may apply).

**Official Portal**: [City of Perth Parking Appeals](https://www.perth.wa.gov.au/council/parking)

## Common Appeal Grounds in WA
- **Broken Ticket Machines**: Photograph the machine screen and any error codes.
- **Medical Emergencies**: Provide a medical certificate or hospital admission record.
- **Vehicle Breakdown**: Provide a receipt from a towing service or roadside assistance (e.g., RAC).
