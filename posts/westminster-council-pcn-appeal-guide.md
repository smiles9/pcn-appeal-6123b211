Westminster is notorious for being the most active parking enforcement area in the UK. If you've received a Penalty Charge Notice (PCN) in central London, you are not alone. However, Westminster also has specific procedural requirements that they must follow.

## Common Grounds for Appeal in Westminster

### 1. Inadequate Signage

Many streets in Westminster have complex parking restrictions that change during the day. If a sign was twisted, spray-painted over, or hidden by a tree, your ticket is invalid.

### 2. The 10-Minute Grace Period

As discussed in our general guide, Westminster MUST observe the 10-minute grace period on all council-run bays.

### 3. Loading/Unloading Exemptions

If you were seen stopping for less than 20 minutes to load or unload heavy goods (and this is visible on CCTV or documented), you have a strong case for cancellation.
