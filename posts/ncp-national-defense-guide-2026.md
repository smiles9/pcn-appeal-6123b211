# Beating NCP in 2026: The Tech Glitch Defense

NCP (National Car Parks) manages some of the most expensive spaces in the UK. Their reliance on the 'PayByPhone' and proprietary apps often creates technical loopholes.

## 1. Frustration of Contract (App Glitches)
If you tried to pay but the NCP app or machine failed, you have a 'Frustration of Contract' defense.
- **Evidence**: Screenshots of 'Error' messages or 'Service Down' status on the day of the ticket.

## 2. The Multi-Story Signage Loophole
In many NCP multi-story car parks, the terms are only displayed on the ground floor. 
- **The Defense**: If you parked on Floor 4 and no signs were visible there, you were never properly notified of the terms.
