# Brighton Center NCP Appeal Guide (2026)

Brighton is notorious for its aggressive parking enforcement. One of the most complained-about sites in early 2026 is the **NCP Brighton Centre** multi-storey car park.

## 1. The 'Payment App Delay' Defense
Many drivers receive fines because the NCP/RingGo app takes 10-15 minutes to process a payment after they have entered. NCP's ANPR cameras log you the second you enter, creating a 'gap' of unpaid time.
- **The BPA Rule**: You are entitled to a reasonable time to pay. 15 minutes is considered reasonable for a faulty or slow mobile app.
- **Your Evidence**: Screenshot your payment confirmation and the 'Attempted Payment' timestamps.

## 2. Signage Visibility at Night
The entry signs at Brighton Centre are frequently obscured by shadows or height. If you entered after 8:00 PM, take a photo of the entrance. If the terms are not legible under street lighting, the contract is void.

## 3. High Value Appeals
Brighton fines often jump from £60 to £170 very quickly. If you've received a 'Final Notice' from a debt collector (like DRP), don't panic. They cannot take you to court without the parking company's permission, which they rarely get for single tickets with strong defenses.
