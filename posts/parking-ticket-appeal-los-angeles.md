# How to Appeal a Parking Ticket in Los Angeles

If you’ve received a parking ticket in Los Angeles (issued by LADOT), you have three stages of appeal. The first stage, **Initial Review**, must be requested within **21 days** of the ticket's issuance.

## Los Angeles Parking Ticket Hotspots
LADOT is extremely active in these high-demand areas:
*   **Venice Beach / Abbot Kinney:** High enforcement for "No Parking" during street cleaning and exceeding time limits at meters.
*   **Hollywood (Walk of Fame):** Heavy monitoring of loading zones and passenger drop-off areas.
*   **Downtown LA (DTLA):** Strict enforcement of "No Stopping" during peak commute hours (7:00 AM – 9:00 AM and 4:00 PM – 7:00 PM).
*   **Westwood (UCLA):** Frequent ticketing for residential permit violations and expired meters.

## How to Dispute Your Ticket
In Los Angeles, the process starts with an **Administrative Review**.

1.  **Online Contest:** Submit your request via the [LADOT Parking Citation Portal](https://ladot.lacity.gov/residents/parking/contest-citation).
2.  **Required Evidence:** Upload clear photos of the scene, your dashboard (showing a valid permit or receipt), and any misleading or obscured signs.
3.  **Statement:** Provide a clear, factual explanation of why the ticket was issued in error.
4.  **Timeline:** You must submit the request within 21 days of the ticket date or 14 days from the mailing of the delinquent notice.

## Common Successful Grounds for Appeal
| Ground | Documentation Needed |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Defective Meter** | Photo of the meter display and the meter serial number. |
| **Signs Not Visible** | Wide-angle photo showing the tree/foliage or object blocking the sign. |
| **Valid Disabled Placard** | Photo of your placard and the identification card (ID) matching the placard. |

## FAQ
|A| Can I ignore an LA parking ticket? ||| No. Unpaid tickets will result in late fees, a DMV hold on your registration, and potential towing if you have 5 or more citations.
|A| How long does the initial review take? ||| Decisions are usually mailed or emailed within 30 to 45 days.
|A| What if my initial review is denied? ||| You can move to the second stage: an **Administrative Hearing**, where you can present your case to a hearing officer (requires payment of the fine first, refunded if you win).
