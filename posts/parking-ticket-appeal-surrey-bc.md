# Surrey, BC Parking Ticket Dispute Guide (2026)

Surrey is one of the fastest-growing cities in Canada, and parking enforcement has scaled accordingly. Whether it's a City of Surrey bylaw ticket or a private fine from Impark or Diamond, here is how you fight back.

## Surrey Parking Hotspots
- **Guildford Town Centre**: High volume of private parking enforcement for time limit violations.
- **Central City Mall / SFU Surrey**: Strict enforcement of student and visitor permit zones.
- **Surrey Memorial Hospital**: Frequent disputes over emergency parking and time extensions.
- **Whalley / City Parkway**: City bylaw enforcement for stopping and loading zones.

## How to Dispute a City of Surrey Ticket
City tickets must be disputed within **14 days** to be eligible for a screening review.
1. **Screening Officer**: First level of dispute where an officer reviews your evidence.
2. **Adjudication**: If the screening fails, you can request a formal hearing (a $25 fee applies if you lose).

**Dispute Portal**: [City of Surrey Parking Services](https://www.surrey.ca/services-payments/parking-tickets)

## Private Parking Fines (Impark/Diamond)
In BC, private parking "invoices" are not the same as government fines. However, they can be sent to collections. 
- **The "No Tow" Strategy**: If you have unpaid private tickets, avoid parking in their lots as they may tow your vehicle.
- **Dispute the Contract**: If the signage was unclear or the machine was broken, photograph it immediately.
