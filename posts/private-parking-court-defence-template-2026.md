# The 2026 'Byelaw Strike' Court Defence Template

## The Skeleton Argument (Template Snippet)
> *"The Defendant avers that the land in question is subject to Airport Byelaws. Therefore, it is not 'relevant land' as defined by Schedule 4 of the Protection of Freedoms Act 2012. Consequently, the Claimant has no statutory right to transfer liability to the registered keeper..."*

Judges are increasingly aware of the "Byelaw Overlap." Parking companies hope you won't show up or won't know the law. Filing a professional **Skeleton Argument** often leads to the claimant withdrawing the case.
