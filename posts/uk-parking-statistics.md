## UK Parking Statistics at a Glance

**The UK parking industry generates over £2 billion annually from tickets and charges, yet more than half of all appeals to independent tribunals succeed — suggesting that hundreds of thousands of tickets are issued incorrectly every year.** These statistics are compiled from public sources, tribunal annual reports, and industry data.

## Enforcement Trends (2020-2026)

- Private parking charges have increased by approximately **300%** over the past decade
- Average charge amounts have risen by **60-80%** since the Beavis ruling (2015)
- ANPR camera deployment has grown by approximately **25% year-on-year**
- Council parking revenue has recovered to pre-pandemic levels after a ~40% drop in 2020-21
- The number of tribunal appeals has increased by approximately 15% year-on-year

## Key Takeaways

1. **More than half of appeals succeed** — the odds are in the motorist's favour
2. **Most private charges never reach court** — only 2-5% of disputed charges are litigated
3. **Procedural grounds are strongest** — late NtKs and invalid TROs have 80-95% success rates
4. **The industry is growing** — private charges have tripled in a decade, making rights awareness more important than ever
