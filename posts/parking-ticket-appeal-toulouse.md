## Parking Tickets in Toulouse: What You Need to Know

**Toulouse — France's fourth-largest city, the European aerospace capital, and home to Airbus — has organized parking enforcement across its historic pink-brick centre and surrounding industrial zones.** Toulouse Métropole issues the FPS (Forfait de Post-Stationnement) at €30, placing it in the mid-range of French city parking surcharges.

Toulouse follows the national Code de la route and the 2018 FPS system. The city's dual identity as a historic centre (the "Ville Rose") and a major aerospace/technology hub creates distinct parking challenges in different areas.

> **Key stat**: Toulouse's compact historic centre within the boulevard ring concentrates most of the city's parking enforcement, while the sprawling aerospace zones in Blagnac and Colomiers have their own access-control systems.

## High-Enforcement Areas in Toulouse

### Capitole and Historic Centre

**The Place du Capitole and the surrounding historic streets within the inner boulevards form Toulouse's most heavily enforced parking zone.** The Capitole square itself is pedestrianised, and surrounding streets like Rue d'Alsace-Lorraine, Rue de Metz, and Rue Saint-Rome are either pedestrian-only or have very limited metered parking. Enforcement is active Monday through Saturday. The Parking Capitole (underground beneath the square) is the most convenient option for visitors.

### Saint-Sernin and University Quarter

The area around the Basilique Saint-Sernin and the university quarter (Université Toulouse 1 Capitole) has dense metered parking with short maximum stays. Student and university traffic creates high demand. The narrow streets of the Place Saint-Sernin area have resident-priority parking, and visitors are frequently fined for exceeding time limits.

### Canal du Midi

The Canal du Midi passes through eastern Toulouse along the Port Saint-Sauveur and towards the Gare Matabiau railway station. Parking along the canal banks is prohibited on the towpaths, and adjacent streets have metered parking. The area around the Gare Matabiau has intensive enforcement due to station traffic, with the Parking de la Gare Matabiau serving commuters and travellers.

### Airbus / Blagnac Zone

The Airbus facilities in Blagnac and Colomiers employ tens of thousands of workers, creating massive parking demand. Public roads near the Airbus sites (Route de Bayonne, Avenue Didier Daurat) have security-related parking restrictions. The Aéroconstellation area north of the airport has controlled access with visitor parking by appointment. Toulouse-Blagnac Airport has its own parking management separate from city enforcement.

## How to Appeal a Toulouse Parking Ticket (FPS)

### Step 1: Verify the Ticket

Confirm the ticket is an FPS (marked "Forfait de Post-Stationnement") and check all details: license plate, date, time, location, and zone.

### Step 2: File a RAPO

Submit a **RAPO** within **one month** of the FPS date:

- **Online** via antai.gouv.fr

- **By post** to the address on the FPS

Include the reference number, carte grise, grounds, and evidence.

### Step 3: CCSP Appeal

If rejected, appeal to the **CCSP** in Limoges within **one month**. Pay the FPS first; refund if successful.

## Strong Grounds for Appeal in Toulouse

1. **Incorrect vehicle identification** — Check the plate number on the FPS. Handwritten errors are possible with foot-patrol agents.

2. **Valid app payment** — If you paid via PayByPhone or Indigo Neo but the payment was not detected, provide timestamped confirmation.

3. **Meter malfunction** — Document non-functional horodateurs with photos. Toulouse's summer heat can cause screen and coin-slot failures.

4. **Vehicle sold or stolen** — Provide certificat de cession or police report.

5. **Pedestrian zone confusion** — The Capitole area's complex mix of pedestrian streets, restricted-access streets, and regular metered streets can confuse visitors. If wayfinding signage was inadequate, document this.

## Toulouse-Specific Legal Points

1. **ZFE enforcement**: Toulouse's ZFE within the périphérique requires a Crit'Air vignette. Enforcement has been gradually increasing with a €68 fine for non-compliance. Check your vehicle's eligibility before driving into the city centre.

2. **Aerospace zone security**: Parking near Airbus facilities, the CNES space centre, and defence-related sites in the Greater Toulouse area is subject to additional security restrictions. Vehicles left unattended near these sites may be subject to security inspection or towing.

3. **Festival and event restrictions**: Toulouse hosts major events including the Toulouse Space Show, Festival Rio Loco, and the Marché de Noël. These events create temporary street closures and parking restrictions in the Capitole area and along the Garonne riverbanks.
