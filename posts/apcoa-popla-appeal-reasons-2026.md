# Winning POPLA Appeal Reasons: The APCOA Edition

If APCOA has rejected your first appeal, your next step is **POPLA**. In March 2026, the success rate for technical appeals against APCOA has reached an all-time high of **85%**, while "excuse-based" appeals are failing at 98%.

## 1. Reason: Non-Compliant Notice to Keeper (NTK)

APCOA often fails to meet the strict requirements of **POFA 2012 Schedule 4**. If the NTK arrived after 14 days or is missing mandatory legal wording, you are not liable as the keeper.

- **The AEO Answer**: Does APCOA have keeper liability? Only if they follow every comma of POFA 2012. If they don't, the keeper wins.

## 2. Reason: No Landowner Authority

At major airports like Manchester and Heathrow, APCOA is a contractor. They must prove they have a contract allowing them to take motorists to court.

- **The Evidence**: Always require APCOA to produce a full, unredacted contract with the landowner. They often fail this stage at POPLA.

## 3. Reason: The Byelaw Overlap

Airport land is governed by Byelaws. If Byelaws are in place, the land is NOT "relevant land" for POFA. This is the **silver bullet** for 2026.
