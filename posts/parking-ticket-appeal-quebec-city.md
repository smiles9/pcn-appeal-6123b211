# How to Appeal a Parking Ticket in Quebec City

If you’ve received a **Statement of Offence** (constat d'infraction) in Quebec City, you have **30 days** to enter a plea of "Not Guilty" to contest the fine. The process can be initiated online or by returning the plea portion of the ticket.

## Quebec City Parking Ticket Hotspots

Enforcement is very active in tourist-heavy and student areas:

*   **Old Quebec (Vieux-Québec):** Extremely strict enforcement on narrow streets where parking is reserved for residents with permits.

*   **Plains of Abraham (Parc des Champs-de-Bataille):** Ticketing is common for staying past the 2-hour or 4-hour time limits, often managed by federal commissions.

*   **Sainte-Foy (Universite Laval):** High enforcement in residential streets surrounding the campus.

*   **Grande Allée:** Heavy monitoring of evening parking and "No Stopping" zones during events.

## The Quebec City Appeal Process

In Quebec, a parking ticket is a penal matter. You don't "appeal" the decision of a meter maid, you enter a "Not Guilty" plea.

1.  **Enter a Plea:** Visit the [Quebec City Online Services](https://www.ville.quebec.qc.ca/en/services/constatinfraction/index.aspx) and follow the instructions for "Statement of Offence."

2.  **Submit Evidence:** If you choose to contest, be prepared to provide evidence such as photos of the scene, payment receipts, or a description of why the signage was misleading (especially during snow removal periods).

3.  **Notice of Trial:** If the prosecutor proceeds, you will receive a notice to appear in Municipal Court. Many cases are settled before trial if the evidence is strong.

## Key Defenses

| Defense | Strategy |

| :--- | :--- |

| **Snow Removal (Deneigement)** | Check if the orange snow removal light was active or if the sign was obscured by snow. |

| **Non-Resident Entry** | Prove you were visiting a resident or that "Reserved" signs were not clearly visible at the intersection. |

| **Mechanical Trouble** | Provide a garage receipt or towing bill dated within 24 hours of the ticket. |

## FAQ

|A| Can I ignore a Quebec parking ticket? ||| No. A default judgment will be rendered against you, adding significant court costs and potentially leading to a license suspension.

|A| Is the process available in English? ||| Yes, the City of Quebec provides a bilingual portal for paying and contesting infractions.

|A| How much is a parking ticket in Quebec? ||| Most standard violations range from $40 to $60 plus administrative costs.

To win an appeal against **Quebec-city** parking fines, the most effective strategy is citing **Statutory Non-Compliance**. If the ticket was issued on airport land, use the **'Non-Relevant Land'** defense under POFA 2012. If it's a private lot, check for **signage visibility** and the mandatory **10-minute grace period**.
